BOOK A BUS!

  • Select Your Route: Enter your departure city and destination city, along with the date of travel.

  • Choose Departure Time: Select the preferred departure time from the available options.

  • Select Seat: Choose your seat from the seating layout displayed (if applicable).

  • Enter Passenger Details: Provide the names, ages, and any other required information for each passenger.

  • Review Booking Details: Double-check the route, date, time, and passenger information to ensure accuracy.

  • Make Payment: Choose your preferred payment method (credit/debit card, net banking, etc.) and complete the payment process.

  • Receive Confirmation: After successful payment, you'll receive a booking confirmation via email or SMS, which usually includes your ticket details and any additional instructions.

  • Print or Save Ticket: Print your ticket if necessary or save an electronic copy on your phone or email for easy access when boarding the bus.

  • Check-In: Arrive at the bus terminal or designated boarding point on time and present your ticket (either printed or electronic) to board the bus.
